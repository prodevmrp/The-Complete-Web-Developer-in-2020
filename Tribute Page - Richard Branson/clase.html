Sr.Richard Branson

Inveterate entrepreneur

Born on July 18, 1950, he started his first venture at age 16, the magazine "Student". In the early 1970s, he opened the first British disco store (in London) and after a while he launched his record label "Virgin Records".

In 1984, Branson launched his transatlantic airline company Virgin Atlantic Airways. From there he launched Virgin Express, Virgin Nigeria, and Virgin America. He merged with the UK-based communications provider NTL in 2006 to form Virgin Media. He also founded the train company Virgin Trains.

The Virgin Group controls over 400 companies, so Branson was knighted (Sir Richard Charles Nicholas Branson) in 2000 for his services to the venture. In 2002, he was chosen among the Top 100 Britons in a BBC poll. He has written several books.

2004, formed Virgin Galactic a space exploration company (owned by Virgin Group). 2006, Richard formed Virgin Comics and Animation (now Liquid Comics), an entertainment company founded with author Deepak Chopra. In 2007 he launched a new company by the name of Virgin Media.

In February 2018, Branson announced the first Virgin Hotel in the UK would open in Edinburgh. Virgin Hotels acquired the popular Hard Rock Hotel in Las Vegas and rebranded it a Virgin Hotel after an extensive renovation.
